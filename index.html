Tutorial

  1. Enter the keyword you want to research.
  2. Select the target location (e.g., select US to view search results for the United States).
  3. Click the "Generate" button.
  4. It is highly recommended to copy the generated URL and paste it into an Incognito/Private Window. This ensures your personal search history and local settings do not influence the results, giving you a neutral view.

Why Use This Tool

Google's search results are heavily customized based on a user's location (gl), language (hl), and browsing history. As an SEO professional, it is critical to see "clean" data to accurately gauge rankings in different target markets.

This tool generates a URL with the gl (geolocation) and pws=0 (Personalized Web Search Off) parameters. These parameters force Google to bypass personal customization, helping you retrieve the most objective and accurate search results for your target region.
